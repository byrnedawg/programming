Welcome

Everyone has ideas but not everyone is an Engineer...
Thats where Byrnedawg Engineering comes in.

My name is Greg Byrne and I’m and Electrical and Computer Engineer who currently works for the Department of Defense at Picatinny Arsenal. I have Masters of Engineering in Computer Engineering from Stevens Institute of Technology and a Bachelor’s of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Rowan University. I have experience in dealing with the full life cycle of a product within the DOD and I feel I can take this knowledge and experience from the DOD world and apply it to the development of consumer products. I personally believe that if you are not always trying to progress your skills and knowledge you end up regressing. This is one of the main reasons I decided to start my own Engineering consulting firm. I wanted the opportunity to work on some projects outside the scope of my daily work and take your ideas and create new innovative products and design solutions.
